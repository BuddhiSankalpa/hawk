F.A.Q

Some important F.A.Q

  • What Are The Qualifications And Requirements I Should Have For This Training?

    Basically the requirement for this is your commitment and interest towards learning about the stock market. Apart from that you need to have Basic English knowledge and Basic computer knowledge. Our team has developed this content in a way that anybody can understand and we will be guiding you throughout the journey. Also you will be needing a laptop/Desktop or a mobile phone as this is a web based training.

  • What Is The Duration Of This Training? What Do We Have To Learn About During This Training?

    Duration of training would be 2-3 months. We will be having 35 hours of theory sessions followed up by 15 hours of practical sessions. During theoretical sessions, we will be teaching about end to end understanding about the stock market. During the practical session, we will be guided to trade with paper money until you become fit to trade with real money. And most importantly we will be teaching you how to manage your money while you are trading and to develop trader’s mindset.

  • How Much Can I Earn After Following This Training?

    First of all Stock trading is not a way of making quick money. In the initial stage we will be guiding you to obtain a profit around 10%-15% of your investment. It’s all depending on the effort that you are putting to understand. Stock trading is not a one time trade, but it’s a journey that you need to practice and learn. Once you understand the procedures, the profit margin can be improved.

  • Don’t You Face Losses While Trading?

    Stock Trading is based on profits and losses. Every trader in this world faces losses. But we teach you how to minimize the losses and to understand a loss before it gets worse. Also once you take our training, you’ll be getting a free subscription until the end of this year of our market analyzing service. We will be providing you market analysis done by DOKS USA. Using this information you can minimize the losses while you are trading.

  • How Do I Join The Training?

    We will be enrolling students in 2 batches for a single month. Each batch would be consist of Maximum 50 students. First you will have to register on our website. Once you get registered, you will be having free content where you can learn more about the New York Stock Exchange as well as the training. While you are referring to the content one of our agents will contact you and guide you with the enrollment.
